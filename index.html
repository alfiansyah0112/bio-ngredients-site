What we stand for

Ingredients with a story you can follow.

We believe you should know the field, the farmer, and the harvest date of every kilo. Our whole process is designed around that simple accountability.

Regeneratively grown

Cover-cropped fields, minimal till, and rotations chosen to rebuild topsoil season after season. Every partner farm is audited yearly.

Gently processed

Cold expeller-pressing, stone milling, and low-temp drying preserve the native proteins, oils, and aromas that harsher processing strips away.

Fully traceable

Every bag ships with a lot code that traces back to the exact field, harvest window, and operator — no blending, no anonymisation.
