What makes it different

Small choices, compounded.

There is nothing radical about cold-pressing sunflower seeds. The difference is that we refuse every shortcut along the way.

De-hulled before pressing

The hull carries most of the fibre, chlorogenic acid, and dark pigments. Removing it first gives a light, almost beige meal that looks and tastes clean in finished applications.

Cold expeller-pressed below 45 °C

No hexane solvent, no high-temperature extraction. Native protein structure stays intact, which means better water-binding and emulsification in your formulation.

Single-origin, single-variety

Every lot comes from one farm, one variety, one harvest window. No blending across seasons or producers — so your supply is as consistent as the field.

Lot-traceable to the row

Every bag ships with a QR code that opens the lot passport: field ID, harvest date, moisture, operator, press temperature. No anonymised commodity blending.
